About

There are thousands of shipwrecks in the Great Lakes and several have known coordinates. This interactive map is perfect for anyone interested in exploring shipwreck locations or to use to find scuba diving sites. The filters allow for narrowing the results by the type of ship, what caused the ship to sink, and lake and the year that it sunk. If you know the name of the shipwreck you are looking for you can use the search feature to easily find its location.
